Wine Guide

Vredebosch Merlot

2018-03-23. (red, dry, screw)

The label reads 14% and I'm pretty sure that's accurate.

My mother put her leg on the table so I could read the size of her shoes on the sole.

Size 37. I said.

Better don't go out when it's stormy. my father noted.

I picked this one up at Lidl for less than 3 Euros.

We cracked it open for dinner and had some chicken wings with salad. The wine definitely fit that very well. The label recommends to have it with grilled meats.

It's a dry wine with a slightly fruity and sour taste. It's very pleasant to drink and definitely reminds me of some nice summer evenings.

I'll rate three things: The price, obviously; How pleasant it is to drink and whether or not the label looks good. Never judge a book by its cover. I don't think that applies to wine, though.
